Kandy View Hotel Ampitiya Road, Kandy

Located amidst the hills of Kandy, this hotel overlooks a rainforest and houses homely accommodation

Queen's Hotel is housed in a historical building located 50 metres from Kandy Lake. It offers colonial-style accommodation with an outdoor pool, 3 dining options and free parking on site.

Amanda Hills is located in Kandy, a 5-minute drive from Kandy City Centre shopping complex. It offers airport pick-up and tour arrangements. Free Wi-Fi is available at the rooftop restaurant.

Situated along the banks of Mahaweli River, Mahaweli Reach Hotel offers 5-star accommodation with rolling mountains as a backdrop. It features a large outdoor pool, Ayurvedic centre fitness.

Amaya Hills Kandy

Located in the Cultural Triangle, Amaya Hills Kandy offers a hillside retreat overlooking Heerassagala Forest.
